Pumpkin Chocolate Chip Cookies

Soft, sweet, and full of warm fall flavor

These pumpkin chocolate chip cookies are easy to make and deliver a soft, cake-like texture with the perfect amount of cinnamon and chocolate. They bake quickly and work great for parties, holidays, or just a cozy evening treat.

Ingredients (tap to add to SaveMart cart)

  • 1 cup canned pumpkin
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon milk
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 teaspoons cinnamon
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts (optional)

Preparation Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C) and line a baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Mix the pumpkin, sugar, oil, and egg in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Dissolve the baking soda in milk and add it to the mixture.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour, cinnamon, and salt.
  5. Stir the dry ingredients into the wet mixture.
  6. Add chocolate chips, vanilla, and walnuts (if using).
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough onto the baking sheet.
  8. Bake 12–15 minutes until the cookies are set around the edges.
  9. Cool and enjoy!
